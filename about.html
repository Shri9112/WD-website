ABOUT KHELO INDIA

The importance of sports and fitness in one’s life is invaluable. Playing sports inculcates team spirit, develops strategic analytical thinking, leadership skills, goal setting and risk taking. A fit and healthy individual leads to an equally healthy society and strong nation.

Sports is an extremely important component for the overall development of our nation. India, in the last few years has made steady progress in the field of sports. This tremendous potential needs to be showcased at a global platform. It’s time we inspire young talent, give them top-notch infrastructure and training of the highest level. We need to inculcate a strong spirit of participation in sports that enables players to demonstrate their true potential. Only then can India realise its dream of becoming a sports super power.

The Khelo India programme has been introduced to revive the sports culture in India at the grass-root level by building a strong framework for all sports played in our country and establish India as a great sporting nation.

TO ACCOMPLISH THE ABOVE OBJECTIVES, KHELO INDIA PROGRAMME HAS BEEN DIVIDED INTO 10 VERTICALS, NAMELY:

  • 1. State level Khelo India centres
  • 2. Annual Sports Competitions
  • 3. Talent search and development
  • 4. Utilization and creation
  • 5. Support to regional/national Academics
  • 6. Physical fitness of children
  • 7. Sports for women
  • 8. Promotion of Sports
  • 9. Sports for peace and development
  • 10. Promotion of indegious/tribe games

A Vision for Sporting Excellence

Khelo India 2.0 is a transformative initiative by the Government of India, aiming to revolutionize sports in the country. The scheme aims to identify, nurture, and develop young talent across all disciplines, fostering a culture of sportsmanship and healthy competition

Key Objectives

The program emphasizes grassroots development, providing access to quality training, facilities, and scholarships. Khelo India 2.0 aims to create a sustainable eccsystem for sports in India, ensuring a bright future for our athletes on the global stage

Introduction

Welcome to Khelo India 2.0, a flagship program of the Indian govemment, dedicated to reviving the sports culture in India at the grass roots level. Our mission is to identify talented young athletes and nurture them to achieve excellence on the national and intemational stage

Focus Areas

Khelo india 2.0 focuses on infrastructure development, community participation, talent identification, coaching and training, and athlete support. Our goal is to make sports an integral part of every indiart's iffe.

National Sports Ecosystem

We strive to build a robust and vibrant sports ecosystem through strategic collaborations, innovative initiatives, and leveraging technology We are committed to enhancing coaching standards, promoting fair play, and ensuring a level playing field for all participants.

Community Engagement

Khelo India 2.0 engages with communities across the nation, encouraging widespread participation in sports. We believe that sports has the power to unite, inspire, and foster a sense of national pride. By making sports accessible to all, we are building a healthier and more active india

Impact and Future

Khelo India 2.0 has made significant strides in promoting sports across india. We are constantly seeking new ways to enhance our training facilities, identify new talents, and provide our athletes with the support and guidance they need to succeed. We are confident that through our efforts, we will continue to see india shine on the global sports stage.

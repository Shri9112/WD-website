"Opportunities for Athletes, Coaches, Volunteers, and More!"

"Unlock opportunities to compete, train, and contribute to India's sporting future."


Khelo India is a flagship program by the Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports, aimed at developing a strong sporting culture in the country. Whether you’re an aspiring athlete, an experienced coach, a dedicated volunteer, or a passionate sports enthusiast, there’s a place for you in this movement. Join us in transforming India into a global sports powerhouse!

INFORMATIVE


ContextKhelo India is a national initiative focused on fostering sporting talent across India. It encourages participation at every level—from school competitions to national championships, and even coaching and volunteering opportunities. By providing platforms for athletes, coaches, and volunteers, Khelo India aims to bridge the gap between potential and performance, ensuring that sports development reaches every corner of the country.

CONTEXTUAL


ContextThe Khelo India initiative includes several sub-programs:
For Athletes: Competitions at school/college and grassroots levels, alongside talent hunts and trials.

For Coaches/Trainers: Certification programs and employment opportunities at sports academies and Khelo India Centers.

For Volunteers: Event management, administrative support, and logistics.

For Experts: Roles for sports scientists, physiotherapists, and technical officials.

These opportunities are interconnected and contribute to the larger goal of making sports accessible and impactful across India.
